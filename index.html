About the Designer

Crafting Stories
Through Fabric

Simi Das grew up surrounded by the rich textile traditions of Bengal, watching her mother drape saris with an effortless elegance that left an indelible mark on her creative soul. From childhood sketches to a degree in fashion design, her journey was always destined for the runway.

In 2021, she founded Simi's Boutique with a singular vision: to create a space exclusively for women — where every piece honours the female form in its diversity, celebrates confidence, and tells a story worth wearing.

Her design philosophy blends Eastern heritage with contemporary minimalism. She believes clothing should feel like a second skin — not a costume — and every Simi's Boutique garment is designed with that intimate intention.
